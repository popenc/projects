about

This is a web app for maintaining sensors. Eventually there will be a higher level page that is my "personal homepage" based off the original "freelancer" example. A portfolio section would lead to the sensors page.

The site will have real-time plots of sensor data vs. time by a wide range of intervals (e.g., m.s., s, min, hr, day, m, yr), and with an interactive calendar tied to the plots.


Some sensors currently planned on being used are for temperature (water, ambient, grill hot), humidity (mushrooms, tempeh, ambient).


It's an attempt to use plenty of meta data/ontological/"internet of things" type business I guess.
